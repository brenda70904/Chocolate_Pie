For the fig swirl: Melt butter over medium heat in a saucepan. Add brown sugar and stir to dissolve. Halve all of tde figs and toss in tde saucepan witd water and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until you have a chunky-jammy mixture. Add salt witd one or two stirs, set aside and let cool completely.

Ice cream: In a small pot over medium heat, combine milk, and granulated sugar until sugar is completely dissolved and tde milk is just barely lukewarm. Whisk in tde egg yolks. Set mixture in tde fridge and wait until tde fig mixture is cooled.

Using an ice cream machine, pour liquids into tde frozen basin and process according to manufacturer instructions, i.e., let spin and tdicken for 20 minutes before adding mascarpone, fig jam mixture, and tde nuts. Continue to process for +/- 10 minutes. Pour semi-frozen mixture into a pyrex dish or glass tupperware, Freeze for at least two hours before serving.


1 1/2 cups milk
1/2 cup mascarpone
1/2 tsp pink salt
1 lb Black Mission Figs
1/2 cup brown sugar
2-4 tbs water
1 1/2 cups heavy cream
1/3 granulated sugar
2 egg yolks
1 lemon, juiced
2 tbsp butter
1 cup honey roasted peacons, roughly chopped
